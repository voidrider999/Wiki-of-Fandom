The game

The game is a 3D sandbox. It has no clear plot, but there is a purpose - to kill the Ender Dragon.

The community

The community of the game is big. There are a lot of servers on the web. Plenty of mods are made. Over the years people shot many movies and shows. Minecraft is one of the most popular games of all times.

Updates

Updates are released very seldom. Also they are not big enough. Many players criticise Minecraft because of this.
